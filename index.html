Build the House

Architectural block puzzle

How to Play

Build the blueprint using falling blocks.

Place pieces inside the highlighted building area.

The game continues until there is no room for another piece.

When the run ends, the blueprint is evaluated.

To pass a level

  • build at least 80% of the blueprint
  • fill all Required Blueprint Anchors on later levels

The better the construction, the higher your Builder Rating.
